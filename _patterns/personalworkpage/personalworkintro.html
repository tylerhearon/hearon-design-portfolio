Personal Projects

I believe that we are always gradually growing and learning. Sometimes, however, we must actively push ourselves outside our comfort zones, and towards new growth, in order to place ourselves closer towards new horizons.

Included here are several personal pieces, completed in the recent years. While they may not fit cleanly into a particular category of art or design, or even have begun as professional projects, these pieces have both educated, and allowed me to express my growth as an artist.
